Future Human

Illustration

2022

"Procreate, Photoshop."

This illustration speculates on future human evolution in response to global warming. With drier climates and increased health issues, women might evolve to lose their breasts to conserve fluids, and human bodies could become larger and flatter to better dissipate heat. To address severe problems with viruses and allergies, humans might develop a “lymph pump” similar to the heart, enhancing the regulation of lymphatic fluid and improving defense against germs and allergens.
